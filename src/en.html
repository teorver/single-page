ABOUT ME Hi. I’m Irina Karpovich. With a university degree in Psychology and over 15 years of experience in interpreting, I provide counselling tailored to support your emotional well-being and personal growth. I’m here to walk alongside you with care and understanding. As a psychologist, I specialize in fixing relationship and family problems for you to get back your happiness and life satisfaction. I strongly believe that it’s much better to have more good days than bad. And due to building strong therapeutic relationships it’s possible to make meaningful progress in mental health and relationships, to learn how to manage emotions, communicate better, and handle stress.
